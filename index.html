Turkey

A state in southeast Europe and southwest Asia, whose culture combines ancient Greek, Persian, Roman, Byzantine and Ottoman traditions. Set on the shores of the Bosphorus, cosmopolitan Istanbul attracts tourists with its iconic Hagia Sophia with its soaring dome and Christian mosaics.
